Introduction to Cloud Computing

Cloud computing allows businesses to access computing resources like servers, storage, databases, and networking over the internet. It provides various levels of abstraction, enabling flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency compared to traditional on-premises infrastructure. This technology empowers organizations to innovate and optimize IT operations with minimal upfront investment

Cloud Services

Cloud services include Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS), providing various levels of abstraction and management of IT resources. These services enable businesses to scale efficiently, reduce operational costs, enhance security, and improve collaboration by leveraging cloud-based solutions tailored to their specific needs
